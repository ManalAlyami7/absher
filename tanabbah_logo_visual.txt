Conceptual Design Elements:

1. The Arabic letter ت (Teh) is stylized with a protective shield shape integrated into its form
2. The ن (Noon) has a subtle eye-like shape in its dot position to represent scrutiny/analysis
3. The ب (Beh) incorporates a subtle warning triangle element
4. The ّ (Shadda) diacritic above the ب is emphasized as an alert symbol
5. The overall typography has a modern, geometric Arabic calligraphy style
6. Green color palette with gradient effects for depth
7. The letters connect in a flowing, contemporary style
8. Negative space is used artistically within the letter forms
9. The entire word forms a protective shield-like shape when viewed as a whole

Color Scheme:
- Primary: #006C35 (Deep green representing Saudi Arabia and security)
- Secondary: #00964A (Lighter green for highlights)
- Accent: #FFFFFF (White for contrast and clarity)
- Background: #F8F9FA (Light background for app icon)

Symbolism:
- The word "تنبّه" itself becomes a symbol of alertness and awareness
- Security elements are subtly integrated into the letter forms
- The green color palette represents safety and trust
- The modern typography conveys technology and AI capabilities

Technical Specifications:
- Scalable vector design suitable for app icons (minimum 16x16px)
- Readable at small sizes
- Works in single color if needed
- Optimized for both light and dark backgrounds
- Aspect ratio suitable for square app icons and horizontal logos

The logo transforms the Arabic word "تنبّه" into a visual symbol that represents the core function of the app - alerting users to potential phishing and fraudulent messages.
